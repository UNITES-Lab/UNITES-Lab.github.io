For Prospective Students

Basic Information
  • The UNITES Lab is actively recruiting PhD students, postdocs, and interns for Fall 2025 and Spring 2026!
  • We are particularly interested in students with strong backgrounds in machine learning, optimization, and AI systems.
  • We always have openings for truly outstanding PhD students, starting at every year's spring and fall semesters (so no need to inquiry "whether you have PhD openings" by email). Our group is well funded, and research assistantships will be provided to PhD students by default.
  • The primary avenue for us to recruit PhD students is through UNC Computer Science. Dr. Chen can advise students across different research areas within CS.
  • Interested candidates for PhD opportunities are strongly encouraged to contact Dr. Chen by email before sending in applications. Please attach your CV, and clearly discuss your research interest and experiences. We value research potential and passion over standardized test scores.
  • Postdocs, and self-funded visitors are welcome to apply, and will be evaluated case by case. We are also open to working with UNC undergraduates and master students for projects. Please contact Dr. Chen by email and specify your interest and relevant background.
What we expect from good candidates?
  • Nothing is more important than a true enthusiasm and devotion to research. We do research only because we are really obsessed with and enjoy our research. Our research interests constantly evolve, and we always stay open to be intellectually excited and inspired by new things.
  • Two other important things that we look for: (1) a truly deep understanding of your problem of interest - don’t naively plug and play "hot" tools; (2) a solid background and a true passion for mathematics. Our research heavily leverages matrix analysis, optimization, and statistical learning.
What we provide to our students?
  • We have a very flat management structure with minimal communication overhead (“Talk is cheap. Show me the code/math”). Every student works directly and closely with Dr. Wang. The group also benefits a lot from its highly interactive, intimate, and helpful culture.
  • We provide extraordinarily strong support to students for internship, visiting, collaboration, networking, and scholarship opportunities. VITA Ph.D. students have in total won nine prestigious fellowships (NSF GRFP, Apple, NVIDIA, Adobe, IBM ×2, Amazon, Qualcomm, and Snap), among many other honors. You are welcome to check their rich experiences.
  • Our students are highly popular among top employers, for both internship and full-time opportunities. During Ph.D. time, almost everyone spends considerable time researching with Google, Meta, Amazon, Apple, NVIDIA, Microsoft, and our many other industry partners, for every year.

About UNC Chapel Hill

A Public Ivy and the nation's first public university, UNC Chapel Hill is one of the most prestigious universities in the United States. Founded in 1789, it is a world-leading research institution in computer science, medicine, and public health. The university is home to distinguished faculty and alumni, including numerous Nobel Prize winners, National Academy members, and leaders in academia and industry.

The Department of Computer Science at UNC is consistently ranked among the top CS programs in the nation. The department has a strong tradition of excellence in artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, and computational biology. UNC CS faculty are leaders in their fields and maintain strong collaborations with industry partners and other top institutions worldwide.

AI/ML at UNC Chapel Hill

UNC Chapel Hill is a leading center for artificial intelligence and machine learning research. The Department of Computer Science has world-renowned faculty conducting cutting-edge research in machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and AI for healthcare. UNC's AI research is supported by major federal grants from NSF, NIH, and other agencies, as well as strong industry partnerships.

The university's interdisciplinary approach to AI research leverages UNC's strengths across multiple departments, including the School of Medicine, School of Data Science and Society, and Department of Statistics and Operations Research. This collaborative environment provides unique opportunities for students to work on real-world problems at the intersection of AI and various application domains. The Research Triangle area, anchored by UNC, Duke, and NC State, is one of the nation's premier technology hubs with strong connections to industry and startups.

About UNC Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at UNC Chapel Hill is consistently ranked among the top computer science programs in the nation. The department has a long history of excellence in research and education, with faculty members who are leaders in their fields and alumni who have gone on to successful careers in academia and industry. The department offers comprehensive graduate programs with strengths in artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, graphics, robotics, and computational biology.

UNC CS is known for its collaborative and supportive environment, where students work closely with faculty on cutting-edge research. The department maintains strong connections with the Research Triangle tech community and has partnerships with major technology companies. Students have access to excellent computing resources and opportunities for interdisciplinary collaboration across the university.

Chapel Hill and the Research Triangle

Chapel Hill is a vibrant college town known for its beautiful campus, excellent quality of life, and thriving cultural scene. The town consistently ranks among the best places to live in the United States, offering a perfect blend of small-town charm and access to world-class amenities. The mild climate allows for year-round outdoor activities, and the area's natural beauty includes numerous parks, trails, and green spaces.

The Research Triangle, anchored by Chapel Hill, Durham, and Raleigh, is one of the nation's premier technology and research hubs. Home to Research Triangle Park (RTP), the area hosts major tech companies, innovative startups, and world-class research institutions. This creates abundant opportunities for internships, collaborations, and career development. The region's diverse economy, cultural offerings, and relatively affordable cost of living make it an ideal place for graduate students and young professionals.

The area offers a rich quality of life with excellent restaurants, arts venues, music scenes, and outdoor recreation opportunities. The proximity to both mountains and beaches, combined with the intellectual vibrancy of three major research universities, creates a unique and stimulating environment for living and learning.
